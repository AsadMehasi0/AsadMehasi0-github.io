Welcome! I'm Asad Mehasi, a dedicated Data Scientist with a strong foundation in Mathematics and Numerical Analysis. My passion for automation and machine learning drives me to create innovative solutions that add value to businesses.

In addition to my extensive experience in R, Python, SAS, Spark, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau, I've made significant contributions to companies like Atlanta Group. At Atlanta Group, I spearheaded the integration of Python models into their existing modeling infrastructure in Radar. Despite the preference for Radar as the deployment platform, I successfully uploaded Python models using a PMML file, ensuring seamless integration of modern machine learning techniques with their traditional infrastructure. Additionally, I developed an automated code for preprocessing within Radar, streamlining the data preparation process and enhancing efficiency.

Currently, I am freelancing, leveraging my expertise to deliver tailored solutions for clients across various industries. My latest project, the PDF Table Extraction Toolkit, exemplifies my commitment to innovation and problem-solving. It showcases my expertise in data preprocessing, text extraction using Optical Character Recognition (OCR), and advanced clustering techniques such as DBSCAN, providing a seamless solution for extracting tabular data from PDF documents.
